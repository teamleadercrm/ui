<Story name="Best practices" />

## Best practices

### Contrast

For optimal contrast, backgrounds have the lightest tint, while text has the darkest tint.

<Box display="flex">
  <Box flex={1} marginRight={2}>
    <StatusLabel color="mint">Do</StatusLabel>
    <Island color="violet" marginTop="3">
      <Heading2 marginBottom={3}>Teamleader</Heading2>
      <TextBody>More business. Fewer chores.</TextBody>
    </Island>
  </Box>
  <Box flex={1} marginLeft={2}>
    <StatusLabel color="ruby">Don't</StatusLabel>
    <Box
      backgroundColor="violet"
      backgroundTint="normal"
      borderColor="violet"
      borderTint="normal"
      borderRadius="rounded"
      borderWidth={1}
      marginTop="3"
      padding={4}
    >
      <Heading2 color="violet" tint="darkest" marginBottom={3}>
        Teamleader
      </Heading2>
      <TextBody color="violet" tint="darkest">
        More business. Fewer chores.
      </TextBody>
    </Box>
  </Box>
</Box>

### Combination

Stay within the same tint when combining text with a color background.

<Box display="flex">
  <Box flex={1} marginRight={2}>
    <StatusLabel color="mint">Do</StatusLabel>
    <Island color="gold" marginTop="3">
      <Heading2 marginBottom={3}>Teamleader</Heading2>
      <TextBody>More business. Fewer chores.</TextBody>
    </Island>
  </Box>
